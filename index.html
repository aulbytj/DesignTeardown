Erica Stanley (@ericastanley) is a Senior Research Engineering Manager at Mozilla, entrepreneur, and tech diversity, equity and inclusion advocate. Working passionately towards gender and multicultural inclusion in tech, Erica co-founded the Atlanta network of Women Who Code, where she leads new developer workshops and organizes monthly tech talks, hack nights, and networking events for women in tech.
